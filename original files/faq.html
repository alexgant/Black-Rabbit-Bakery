Frequently Ask Questions

Frequently Ask Questions

How Much do you charge?

Pricing varies depending on the customization in the order. Most cakes start at about $45 and will increase in price depending on size, frosting, filling, flavors, etc. There are a few items that have a set price, please check the services page for more information. If you'd like a free quote, please used the Contact form to send a message.

Where are you located

Black Rabbit Bakery is located in Moscow ID. It is ran out of the owners house, so there is no store-front. Please visit the About Us page for more information.

Do you have Sales?

Black Rabbit Bakery occassionally has sales for specific items and quantites, usually for certain holidays. Please check the Sales page for more information.

Do you deliver?

We do! We can deliver to the Moscow/Pullman area for free and surrounding areas for a delivery fee (which varies depending on milage). Please see the About Us page for specifics to towns that we deliver to.

How long does it take to make an order?

Orders must be placed at least 2 weeks in advanced. An order is placed once you have sent a message and the message has been responded to. It may take a day or two for the owner to reply. If your request is urgent please put "URGENT:" in the title of your message. Depending on the order, it usually takes about a week for the order to be started and delivered.

Can you ship?

Currently there is no shipping options. We are not currently set up to ship baked goods in a way that would garuntee freshness or no damage.
