About Black Rabbit

Moscow ID Cake Commissions

Black Rabbit Bakery is owned and operated in Moscow ID. Cakes and other baked goods are baked in the owners home and delivered to customer. Currently commissions can be delivered to the Moscow-Pullman area for free, as well as surrounding town such as Colfax, Colton, Union Town, Lewiston, and Clarkston for an additonal delivery free.

Black Rabbit Bakery specializes in Cake and cupcake commissions but can also do a variety of bakes goods from pies, cookies, ice cream, etc.

Alexis Gantz

Owner and Creator

Alexis Gantz started baking more seriously at the age of 19 and has studied the chemistry of baking as to perfect her recipes. Over the years she has experimented with different flavor combinations as to expand her baking options. She has a bachelores degree in graphic design and is currently working on a web development degree as designing and creating is her passion.

She started Black Rabbit Bakery in January of 2019, which was called "Alexis's Cakes" at the time before switching names. She has lived in Moscow ID for 4 years now, and before that she grew up in Lewiston
